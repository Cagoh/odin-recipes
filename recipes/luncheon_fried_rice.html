Luncheon Fried Rice!

Ingredients

  • 400g overnight cooked rice
  • 3 eggs
  • 100g french beans
  • 3 tbsp light soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp oyster sauce
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil
  • 3 cloves of minced garlic
  • Salt and white pepper to taste
  • 3 tbsp for cooking

Steps

  • Wash and cook 1 cup of rice with water according to instructions on the package.
  • Cut french bean and luncheon meat into smaller pieces.
  • Beat 3 eggs. Add 1 tbsp soy sauce and Â¼ tsp white pepper.
  • In a pan heat 1 tbsp oil, add eggs and scramble. Remove and set aside.
  • In the same pan, add 2 tbsp oil and cook luncheon meat until golden brown.
  • Remove from pan and set aside.
  • Stir fry minced garlic until fragrant, add in french beans, cook for another minute.
  • Add in rice overnight cooked rice, then season with white oyster sauce, light soy sauce ,sesame oil and pepper powder. Stir fry and mix evenly.
  • Add in luncheon meat and eggs. Mix well and serve.
